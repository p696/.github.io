                                                                   Coronavirus Update

The Legion monthly meeting on May 5th has been scheduled. This meeting will be a meeting overview of Post 484 CURRENT Post status, reopening plans and future event cancellations. Dependent on the weather, this meeting may be held outside in Post parking lot. Ohio restrictions to distance compliance and masks will be enforced, and there will be “NO” loitering prior to meeting or after the meeting in the Post.

Information regarding all other MAY events and activities are posted within Auxiliary and Legion Bugler newsletters and calendars. If you have questions regarding other Post activities please contact me at s_ervin49@yahoo.com . 
All Post events are still cancelled during the month of May. Please keep in touch with members who cannot get out an around, contact members by Email or Phone or Facebook, its times like this we need to reach out to all members.
Any changes to Post 484 event or activity status will be posted on this website, Post Facebook, or phone calls to members.

For God and Country
Steve Ervin, Commander
