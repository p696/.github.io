                                        HOSPITALIZED VETERANS

All of the legion family organizations work throughout the year with hospitalized veterans, both at the VA Hospital and at the post. We do this with games, food, and gifts during the holidays and throughout the year. There are Horse Race Games, Bingo, meals, and other events.  The biggest events are Christmas fruit baskets organized by the Auxiliary and distributed at the main VA facility and a big Fourth of July Party, organized by the Sons of the Legion  and also held at the main facility.
