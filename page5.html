What do we do with a worn out flag?  Bring it to Post 484 where it will be properly disposed of in a way prescribed by the U.S. government. We do this once each year on Flag Day, June 14th.  You can bring flags to the post at any time of year.  We will store them until Flag Day.  The Disposal Ceremony is shown below, and all are welcome to attend.

Group is assembled for disposal cermony
 A flag, representing all of the flags, is inspected by several post officers for wear and condition.
That flag, after being found worn out, and all other worn out flags are then burned. (Government preferred method of disposal)
