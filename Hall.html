                                                     HALL RENTAL 

Post 484 has a very nice hall which is used for our meetings and other functions. It also can be rented for many purposes. It has a well equipped kitchen, a stage for a band or DJ, a raised area often used by the bride and groom and wedding party at a wedding reception. There is a small bar, which can serve soft drinks, beer and liquor. The hall is wi-fi equipped for use at meetings and classes.  The hall also has a large parking lot.
