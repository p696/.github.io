On the Saturday before Memorial day, 484 family members, aided by members of the Cub Scout Troop sonsored by the Sons of the Legion place flags on the graves of about 500 veterans.  On Memorial Day we visit the cemetaries which contain these graves to honor those brave Service men and women who lost their lives fighting for our freedom. Our Honor Guard (comprised of legion and SAL members) and other interested persons visit the following cemetaries: Mt Washington, Guardian Angels, Mt. Moriah, Five Mile Chapel, and Hillcrest.  At each of them we have a remembrance service followed by a 21 gun salute and taps.  We also visit the the Veterans Memorial at McNicholas High School (over the  years, five McNick graduates have died for their country) A short parade ends at the Post 484 Veterans Monument; Sutton Avenue is closed, and a crowd gathers while we hold our traditional Memorial Day service.
